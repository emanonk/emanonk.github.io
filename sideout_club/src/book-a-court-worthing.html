Opening Times & Bookings

Prior to 1 April, our courts are available between 10:00am and 9:30pm on weekdays and 10:00am and 4:30pm on weekends.

From 1 April to 30 September, our courts are available to book between 10:00am and 10:00pm on weekdays. Weekend bookings are not available, as we run different events each weekend. Check out the events calendar here!

Courts can be booked up to 30 days in advance. For any block bookings beyond 30 days, corporate bookings or other private events, please feel free to contact us via the form at the bottom of this page!

Frequent Question

How do I join the Club?

It's simple! Go to our 'Registration' page and: 

1. Log in to your profile, or create one for free if you don't already have one.

2. Then, click on the blue "Become a Member now" button and follow the instructions. The cost is just £21 and that's it - you are officially a Member of the Club! 

3. If you would also like to add on one of the Social, Open Group Training or Private Group Training add-on packages in Leyton or Worthing, take your pick from your profile homepage and submit the form!

What does Membership include?

Membership of the Club includes discounts on events and court hire, access to our tournaments (which are open only to Members or holders of Single Event Passes) and more - in both Leyton and Worthing! Then, depending on your chosen add-on package (Social, Open Group Training or Private Group Training), you get loads of other extra benefits!

See the graphic on the right of this page, as well as the 'Registration' page for full details of Membership and each add-on package. 

When will I train?

If you decide to sign up for the Open Group Training or Private Group Training package, you will train twice per week at your choice of either SideOut Beach in Leyton or SideOut Beach in Worthing. All training groups will train either on Mondays & Wednesdays or on Tuesdays & Thursdays, with training starting running at 18:30-20:15 on one day and at 20:15-22:00 on the other day. In other words, all training groups will have one 18:30-20:15 session and one 20:15-22:00 session per week.  

​

The exact schedule for all groups will be determined as the groups are finalised after the first few sessions of the season. 

What does it cost?

2019 Membership is £21. The add-on packages are charged in addition to the £21 membership fee as follows: 
- Social £75 per person; 
- Open Group Training £245 per person; and

- Private Group Training £2,200 per private group of max. 8 people*.

* A private group can be made up of any number of people up to a maximum of 8, but the total price for the group will always be £2,200. If you would like to have more than 8 people in your private group, please drop us a line! 

​

These can be paid by bank transfer, PayPal, or Direct Debit (up to 4 x equal monthly payments with full amount payable by 5th July 2019).

To pay by PayPal: follow the link provided when registration is complete. 

To pay by bank transfer: transfer the full amount to the SideOut Beach Volleyball Club bank account (details on the registration page) and email us to confirm payment has been made.

To pay by Direct Debit: email us to let us know you would like to pay by Direct Debit and we will provide you the details ASAP!

What happens next?

Once you've signed up for Membership and made your payment, your Membership of the Club will become valid immediately. We recommend downloading the PDF of your Membership Card and storing it on your smart phone. You will then be able to sign up for training and tournaments and take advantage of all of the benefits! If you have also added on one of the Open Group Training or Private Group Training packages, we will contact you directly by email to give you further information about your training sessions.
