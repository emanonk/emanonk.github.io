SideOut Grand Prix



SideOut's Elite Series in London and the South Coast

The SideOut Grand Prix is a series of elite beach volleyball tournaments taking place in London and Worthing throughout the summer.

Featuring full international rules, prizes and much more, the SideOut Grand Prix is a showcase of top level men's and women's beach volleyball. See you on the sand!

Key features:

- 4-star events on the UK Beach Tour;

- Maximum 12 teams per gender;

- Entry determined by ranking points;

- Full scoring (21-21-15);

- Prizes and trophies for the top 3 teams.

    • Sunday 21 July 2019 (men and women)
    • Sunday 1 September 2019 (men and women)

    • 13 July 2019 (men only)

TIMES
  • Approx. 9:00am-7:00pm.
 
​
FORMAT
  • Pool play, followed by single elimination rounds. 
  • Full international scoring (21-21-15).
  • Minimum 3 matches per team.​

 

ENTRY RULES
  • Register and pay online via registration.sideout.co.uk.
  • Registration closes on the Monday before each tournament. 
  • Maximum 12 teams per gender.
  • If more than 12 entries are received, teams will be ranked by ranking points and the top 12 ranked entrants will be eligible to participate.
  • In case of a tie in ranking points, previous results in SideOut tournaments will also be taken into consideration.
  • Up to 2 wildcards per gender may be granted in exceptional circumstances and at SideOut's absolute discretion. If wildcards are granted, only the top 10 or 11 ranked teams (depending on number of wildcards) will be eligible.
