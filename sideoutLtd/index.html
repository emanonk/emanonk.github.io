About SIDEOUT LTD

SideOut Ltd is an organisation comprised of a team of dedicated and highly experienced volleyball and beach volleyball professionals with the aim of contributing to the development of both sports through the provision of top quality coaching, events and playing opportunities for participants of all age groups, abilities and levels of experience.

WHAT WE DO

EVENT MANAGEMENT

Having managed and delivered over 120 local, national and international volleyball and beach volleyball events, our Event Management Division have the knowledge and experience to take on all aspects of volleyball and beach volleyball events, from the early planning stages through to delivery and management.

The Event

The Action For Kids Beach Volleyball Championships take place over three weeks at Wood Wharf in the heart of Canary Wharf and feature hundreds of tonnes of specially shipped sand forming 2 beach volleyball courts, accompanied by lots of side events and activities!

The event includes the SideOut Invitational Tournament featuring some of the best players in the UK, as well as the corporate tournament where teams from some of the biggest businesses in Canary Wharf and the City battle it out on the sand with stiff competition meeting great enterrtainment and a fantastic day out!
